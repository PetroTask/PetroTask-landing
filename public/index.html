What is PetroTask?

PetroTask is a digital platform designed to optimize task management and processes in the oil sector. With an intuitive interface and specialized tools, facilitates planning, monitoring and control of operations at all stages of the logistics chain.

Why was PetroTask created?

The Oil industries face constant challenges: large-scale projects, distributed teams and the need for precision in each stage. PetroTask is born in response to those demands, offering a technological solution that connects teams, centralizes information and improves operational efficiency.

PetroTask Features

Tasks and projects management

Create tasks with description, priority and resources; assign technicians or teams in a few clicks.

Real-time rescheduling

Adjusts the plan in case of unforeseen events and reorganize priorities instantly.

Offline mode and automatic synchronization

The technicians work offline; the data is synchronized when there is a network connection.

Logs and evidences

Task start/end, photos and logs are linked to each task for auditing purposes.

Automatic alerts and push notifications

Set up conditions that trigger alerts and receive security notifications.

Dashboards and logs

Complete visibility of the status of each task, compliance metrics and exportable records.

Security and traceability

Complete record of operations with evidences and access control.
